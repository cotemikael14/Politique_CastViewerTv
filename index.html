# Privacy Policy for CastSender **Last Updated: May 22, 2025** ## Introduction Welcome to CastSender ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our CastSender application on Meta Quest devices ("App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application. ## Information We Collect ### Information You Provide Directly When using CastSender, you may provide: - **Network Configuration**: IP addresses and port numbers for establishing connections - **Quality Settings**: Your preferred streaming quality preferences - **QR Code Data**: Information scanned through QR codes for quick connection setup ### Information Collected Automatically When you use our App, we automatically collect: - **Device Information**: Meta Quest device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers - **Usage Data**: Features used, session duration, and error logs - **Network Information**: Local IP address and network status (WiFi connectivity) - **Performance Metrics**: Frame rates, bitrate, and connection quality statistics ### Permissions We Request CastSender requests the following permissions: - **Camera**: Used solely for QR code scanning to simplify connection setup - **Network Access**: Required for WebRTC streaming functionality - **Foreground Service**: Maintains screen capture session while streaming - **Notification**: Displays streaming status and allows quick access to controls ## How We Use Your Information We use the collected information for: - **Service Delivery**: Establishing and maintaining peer-to-peer connections for screen sharing - **Quality Optimization**: Adjusting streaming quality based on network conditions - **Technical Support**: Diagnosing and resolving technical issues - **App Improvement**: Understanding usage patterns to enhance features ## Data Storage and Security ### Local Storage Only - All configuration data is stored locally on your Meta Quest device - No user data is transmitted to our servers - Connection data is used only for establishing peer-to-peer connections ### Peer-to-Peer Communication - Screen sharing occurs directly between devices using WebRTC - No video content passes through our servers - Connection metadata (IP addresses) is used only for session establishment ### Security Measures - All data transmissions use industry-standard encryption protocols - No passwords or sensitive personal information is collected - Local data can be cleared by uninstalling the app ## Data Sharing and Disclosure We do not: - Sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties - Store your streaming content or connection history - Share your data with advertisers or marketing companies We may disclose information only if: - Required by law or legal process - Necessary to protect our rights or safety - You provide explicit consent ## Children's Privacy CastSender is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information from our systems. ## Third-Party Services CastSender uses the following third-party services: - **WebRTC**: For peer-to-peer video streaming (no data collected by third parties) - **Google STUN Servers**: For NAT traversal (no personal data transmitted) ## Your Rights and Choices You have the right to: - **Access**: View the data stored locally on your device through app settings - **Delete**: Remove all app data by uninstalling CastSender - **Opt-out**: Disable specific permissions through Meta Quest system settings - **Control**: Choose which devices to connect to and when to share your screen ## Data Retention - Configuration settings are retained until you modify or delete them - No streaming content is recorded or retained - Error logs are kept locally for 7 days to assist with troubleshooting - All data is permanently deleted upon app uninstallation ## International Users CastSender is designed to work globally. By using our App, you consent to the processing of your information as described in this Privacy Policy, regardless of your country of residence. ## Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by: - Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy - Providing in-app notifications for significant changes - Requiring your acceptance of updated terms when necessary ## Contact Us If you have questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: **Email**: mxmk@gmail.com **Developer**: MXMK ## Meta Quest Specific Provisions ### Oculus/Meta Platform - We comply with Meta's platform policies and guidelines - User data is not shared with Meta beyond standard platform analytics - We do not access Meta account information beyond device identification ### VR-Specific Considerations - No motion or spatial tracking data is collected - Hand tracking data (if used) remains on-device - No biometric or health-related information is collected ## Consent By using CastSender, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
